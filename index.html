Discover the Taste of Real Coffee

Kenyan coffee is grown mainly in high altitude areas of Kenya including Nyeri, Muranga, Kirinyaga, Kiambu, Meru, Embu and Machakos. Other areas include Western regions, Rift Valley, and Nyanza regions. Coffee is carefully handpicked, selecting only the ripe red cherries, which are then dried. After harvest, beans are graded by size and shape to ensure only the finest quality is selected. Kenyan coffee is ranked among the best in the world, perfect for cappuccino, espresso, and filter coffee.

About Kimani Coffee Experts

Kimani coffee expert ltd is a company that specializes in coffee trade /proffessison coffee roasting services and coffee buying advisory services. we are licensed as coffee dealers by the Agriculture and Food Authority (AFA) -coffee directorate.

The founder of Kimani Coffee Experts Limited is Samuel Mwithukia Kimani, a certified coffee expert. He worked with international coffee companies for close to 28 years in charge of quality.

He took early retirement in 2012 to found Kimani Coffee Experts Ltd, incorporated in May 2013. On 4th December 2013, the company was issued with a coffee trading licence by the Coffee Board of Kenya.

Since then, Kimani Coffee Experts Ltd has been buying, processing, and selling coffee. In 2013, the company bought 2000 bags of coffee from farmers and sold them to local and international buyers.
