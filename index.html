About Kimani coffee

For the last 6 years, Kimani Coffee Experts Limited has specialized in coffee beans from Kenya farmers, processing them to meet global coffee standards. They have focused on the best of coffee grades AA, AB, and PB which they have been exporting to South Korea, Japan, North America, and Europe.

Mr. Samuel Kimani, the founder, is a certified coffee expert with over 30 years of experience in the coffee industry.

“Our coffee beans come from all over Kenya, a country famed for its best coffee exports used to improve the quality of other coffees from other parts of the world. Some of the counties whose farmers we buy from include Nyeri, Kirinyaga, Murang’a, and Meru; basically the Central Mount Kenya and Rift-Valley regions.”
